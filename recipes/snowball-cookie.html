Snowball Cookies

Description

These cookies are such a hit during the holidays. In my house, we have a tradition where we make them every time it snows. We nicknamed them "Snow Cookies". I hope you enjoy making great memories with this recipe as well!

These cookies are easy to make gluten free or vegan as needed by substituting with your favorite gluten-free flour or vegan butter alternative.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened at room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • toasted pecan halves
  • 2 1/4 cups AP flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • powdered sugar for rolling

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients and materials, then preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Beat butter on med high speed in an electric mixer, slowly pour sugar in and beat until butter is a light yellow color and looks fluffy.
  3. Turn speed to low and careful pour vanilla extract into the butter and sugar mixer. Set aside.
  4. Chop or pulse pecans in a food processor to get the desired size. Very small pieces will result in a cookie texture kind of like a "Sandie". Larger pieces will result in a crisp cookie with nice chunks of nuts. Both styles are delicious!
  5. Add the nuts to a separate bowl with the flour and salt
  6. On low speed, turn the butter mixture back on and slowly add the dry ingredients. Mix just until combined, scraping the sides and bottom.
  7. Roll dough into 1 1/2 inch balls and bake them on an ungreased cookie sheet. Leave about 2 inches between each cookie. Cookies will take 12-15 minutes to bake to a pale golden color.
  8. Once cookies are slightly cool (but still warm) roll them in a bowl of powdered sugar. Place on a wire rack to cool completely, then roll in the sugar one last time. Enjoy the Snow Cookies!
